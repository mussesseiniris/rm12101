About Designer/Developer

用艺术表达生活的深度与美感

Hi, my name is Si Zhang, you can call me Iris. I came from China and got my bachelor’s in landscape architecture. For the past decade, I’ve dedicated myself to this field—using drawings and models to turn empty, barren spaces into public parks or green areas where people can relax and hang out. These experiences have equipped me with systematic thinking skills and efficient collaboration capabilities.

My passion stems from two things: problem solving and value creation. The unique charm that programming brings is that it can validate ideas at a very low cost, and I am addicted to the process of realizing interactive logic with code. Every successful debugging is like solving a puzzle, which makes me realize the perfect combination of logic and creativity. In addition, developing programs for people to use through code programming makes me feel that I have created value.
